I am currently working as a PostDoc in the field of visualization research at University of Bamberg, Germany. My research interests include **data visualization**, **game analytics**, and **artificial intelligence (AI)**. I explore and invent techniques to analyze complex data using effective visual encodings. I often apply these visualizations in practical use-cases scenarios, including those related to machine learning. For instance, understanding what AI models have learned. My contributions have been awarded at visualization academic forum as best paper award, and **NeurIPS** AI competitions.

My mission is to advance the state-of-the-art in visual analytics and to create impactful solutions that can help various domains and applications. I am passionate about finding new problems, generating new ideas, and delivering high-quality results. **Looking for the next adventures, I want to use my applied research skills to a relevent role at industry.** If my profile seems interesting or you know of a suitable role, please contact me (LinkedIn or e-mail: shivamworking[at_the_rate]gmail[dot]com).
