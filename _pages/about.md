I am a PhD candidate at University of Duisburg-Essen, Germany. My research interests include **data visualization**, **game analytics**, and **artificial intelligence (AI)**. I am interested in finding effective visual representations of the data enabling exploration of patterns and valuable insights. I often apply these visualizations in practical use-cases scenarios and work with different AI communities, e.g., in *AICrowd* for crowdsourced AI development, *Pommerman* for analyzing AI agent gameplay behavior, etc. The visualization contributions were received well and awarded by the communities.

I am happy to connect, collaborate, and chat about data visualization. Please feel free to write me an e-mail: shivam[dot]agarwal[at_the_rate]uni-due[dot]de

**Keywords**: data visualization, visual analytics, dynamic set/graph visualization, artificial intelligence (AI), game analytics, AI playtesting, and learning analytics.
