I am currently working as a PostDoc in the field of visualization research at University of Bamberg, Germany. My research interests include **data visualization**, **game analytics**, and **artificial intelligence (AI)**. I am interested in finding effective visual representations of the data, enabling exploration of patterns and valuable insights. I often apply these visualizations in practical use-cases scenarios and work with different AI communities, e.g., in *AICrowd* for crowdsourced AI development, *Pommerman* for analyzing AI agent gameplay behavior, etc. The visualization contributions were received well and awarded by the communities.

I am happy to chat, connect, and collaborate. Please feel free to write me an e-mail: shivam[dot]agarwal[at_the_rate]uni-bamberg[dot]de

**Keywords**: exploratory data analysis, information visualization, visual analytics, dynamic set/graph visualization, artificial intelligence (AI), and game analytics.
